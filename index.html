How to play?

Young Timmy is happily walking through town. But it seems he won't be having a nice day. Unless… you do something about it! After all, your an all-mighty god…

Our protagonist, Timmy, is a cheerful, easily-distracted kid, who is walking around the town in a not so careful manner. On his way, there are a great deal of threats to his farewell. There's no reason to worry though, because you, as an all-mighty god of great benevolence, will take care of the boy, deactivating the dangers that show up on the street. In order to do that, you're meant to click or touch to disable them, being careful not to enable pre-deactivated ones. Threats will have very different forms, sizes and behaviours, such as a dangerous dog or a hole in the street. Plus, they may appear in different zones of the town: the sky, the floor, directly in front of Timmy, etc. As a result, you will have to be careful and pay close attention, as well as show great coordination and reflexes. No need to worry though, that should be an easy task for a god such as you!

Will Timmy be able of enjoying his calm walk around the town, or will he fall to the dangers that lurk behind every corner of it? It's up to you!
